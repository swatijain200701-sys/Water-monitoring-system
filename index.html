How This System Works

A simple explanation of what's happening behind the scenes.

1

Sensor Reads Flow

A YF-S201 water flow sensor on the pipe counts how much water passes per minute. Here, it's simulated by the backend.

2

Controller Evaluates

The Node.js server (or a real ESP32 chip) runs the algorithm to decide: is this normal use, high usage, or a leak?

3

Alert Triggered

If water flows non-stop for more than 15 seconds, the system treats it as a leak and fires a red alert immediately.

4

Valve Auto-Closes

A solenoid valve receives the "CLOSE" command and physically shuts off the water supply — stopping the waste automatically.

Design Thinking Principles

Our approach to solving the water wastage problem efficiently.

Empathize & Define

Recognized the silent loss of millions of liters of clean water due to unnoticed leaks and unmonitored human usage in homes and industries.

Ideate

Designed a preventative logic model that doesn't just display data, but actively intervenes (auto-shutoff) to solve the problem before damage occurs.

Prototype & Test

Built a robust scalable WebSockets backend connected to an intuitive dashboard, ensuring zero-latency monitoring simulating real IoT conditions.
